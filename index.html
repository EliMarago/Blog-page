11 Ways to improve Website UX/UI

UX DESIGNEDITORIAL - 4 MINUTES READ

UX is the overall experience of using website or service

UI is that the user click or taps on to do something.
The terms UX and UI are often used interchangeably.
They're so closely related that it can be difficul to appreciate that there's a difference, especialy if this is a side of technology that you don't eat and sleep.So before proceeding, let's define them.

Remember: you have competition and safest way of proceeding is to assume that your competitors are doing everything that you could be doing, but aren't

So let's work through some of the key elements of UX and UI to help your understand the principles behind improving your website
